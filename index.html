About Operation Smile

Every three minutes a child is born with a celft lip. The children who survive are often unable to eat, speak, socialize or smile. And in too many cases, their parents can't afford to give them the surgeries they need to live a productive life. Our goal is to fundraise and provide Operation Smile with funds to provide these children with a smile.

Why We Exist

We dream of a world where no child suffers from lack of access to safe surgery. Learn why we do what we do in the video below.

How the University of Minnesota Helps

As Operation Smile UMN, we will coordinate fundraising events for the Operation Smile program. All the money raised will be directly donated to the program and used in order to fund oral surgeries of underprivileged children around the world. Every child deserves a healthy and beautiful smile.
